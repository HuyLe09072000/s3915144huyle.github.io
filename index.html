Description:
In the second week of study, two lessons provide knowledge related to the construction of argument structures, the synthesis of ideas, effective summary speech, and the methods of persuasive presentations. In topic 4, Dr. Jonathan Crellin guided students to develop technical report writing structures that support science and engineering disciplines. Furthermore, the structures formed will be a key element in supporting the presentation exercise and the future discipline. Students took an extensive look at the audience's standard, compared slides to get a solid idea of the situation and meticulously prepared presentations.
